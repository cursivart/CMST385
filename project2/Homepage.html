    Swipe Right


    The Library focus on a section where a visitor can compare and contrast different VPN services based on their needs. They can compare things like price, ease-of-use, and more. Below are a few notes and an early interface that aims to allow users to "swipe right(add to compare/contrast) or left(push to back of deck)" the different VPN services the website features.
  • Each VPN's card will include simple, star-based evaluations of each of the metrics connected to their services.
  • Once the user has gone through the entire stack and chosen a few key cards, they can then compare the cards in a compare/contrast Spotlight station further down the page.

    Reviews
    Reviews will of course be made available on this page. This will include star-based, filter-driven interface.

    Proposed logo @ top-left is handmade in Adobe Illustrator. I may choose to animate it so it can have a hover animation, but for now, I'm happy with it. I am planning on having the navigation bar slide out from the top margin when the logo is clicked. The target look should be reminiscent of a manilla file folder. This navigation will include the aforementioned Landing Page, Library and Reviews pages, as well as a Legal page, which will just include some brief background about the legality of VPN's, maybe a map of where they are legal and how to use them ethically.
