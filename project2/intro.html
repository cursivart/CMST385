    Swipe Right


    The Library focus on a section where a visitor can compare and contrast different VPN services based on their needs. They can compare things like price, ease-of-use, and more. Below are a few notes and an early interface that aims to allow users to "swipe right(add to compare/contrast) or left(push to back of deck)" the different VPN services the website features.
  • Each VPN's card will include simple, star-based evaluations of each of the metrics connected to their services.
  • Once the user has gone through the entire stack and chosen a few key cards, they can then compare the cards in a compare/contrast Spotlight station further down the page.
